Frequently Asked Questions

How much does balcony cleaning cost in Toronto? expand_more
Our balcony cleaning services start at $229 for the Silver package (under 50 sq ft). The Gold package starts at $329 and includes full glass cleaning inside and out. Our premium Platinum package starts at $449 with nano-coating and a 30-day touch-up guarantee. Balconies over 50 sq ft have adjusted pricing.
What areas in Toronto do you serve? expand_more
We serve all major Toronto neighborhoods including CityPlace, Liberty Village, Harbourfront, King West, Yorkville, The Annex, Midtown, North York, Scarborough, Etobicoke, and Mississauga. We specialize in high-rise condo balconies across the GTA.
How long does a balcony cleaning take? expand_more
Most balcony cleanings are completed in 1–2 hours depending on the package and balcony size. Our technicians arrive with specialized equipment designed for high-rise balconies and premium glass finishes.
Can I book a same-day balcony cleaning? expand_more
Yes! We offer same-day booking with a $75 surcharge for urgent requests. Select today’s date when booking and we’ll do our best to accommodate your schedule.
Do you offer balcony tiling and net installation? expand_more
Yes! In addition to cleaning, we offer balcony tiling services (porcelain, composite decking, natural cedar, rubber safety tiles) and net & screen installation (pigeon nets, insect screens, child & pet safety nets, privacy screens).
Is balcony cleaning safe for high-rise condos? expand_more
Absolutely. Our technicians are trained in high-altitude safety protocols and use specialized equipment designed for high-rise environments. We follow all building management guidelines.
